Data Scientist

Company: TechInnovate Solutions
Location: Remote (US-based)

About the Role:
We are seeking a skilled Data Scientist to join our growing analytics team. In this role, you will work with cross-functional teams to identify and solve complex business problems using data-driven approaches. You will be responsible for developing and implementing advanced machine learning models, conducting statistical analyses, and communicating insights to stakeholders.

Required Qualifications:
- Master's degree or higher in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in data science or related role
- Strong programming skills in Python
- Experience with machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn
- Proficiency in SQL and experience working with large datasets
- Knowledge of statistical analysis and modeling techniques
- Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI, or matplotlib)
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Preferred Qualifications:
- PhD in a quantitative field
- Experience with NLP and deep learning techniques
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Experience with big data technologies (Hadoop, Spark)
- Background in A/B testing and experimental design
- Experience in the tech industry or similar fast-paced environment
- Contributions to open-source projects or research publications

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement machine learning models to solve business problems
- Conduct exploratory data analysis to identify patterns and insights
- Create and maintain data pipelines for model training and deployment
- Collaborate with engineering teams to implement models in production
- Design and analyze A/B tests to measure the impact of new features
- Communicate findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Stay current with the latest developments in machine learning and data science
- Mentor junior data scientists and analysts

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Remote-first work environment with flexible hours
- Professional development opportunities
- Collaborative and innovative team culture
- Opportunity to work on challenging problems with real-world impact
